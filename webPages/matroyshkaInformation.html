These hand-painted wooden nesting dolls are a classic gift from the Czech Republic. We are able to nest 3-10 dolls inside of the outer doll-shell. As the dolls get smaller, marvel at painter Elspeth's fine-motor skills--no matter how small the tiniest doll at the center of the doll-nest is, we guarantee it will have facial features and clothing details!
