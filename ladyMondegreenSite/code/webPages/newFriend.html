Become an Eggcorn

Welcome to the patronage form! Lady Mondegreen and her Eggcorn Consort thank you for considering becoming one of their patrons. Many noblefolk across the country are patrons of our ensemble, and we would be honored to add you to their number!

Patrons of Lady Mondegreen and the Eggcorns get to enjoy one house concert every year, free of charge. You will also recieve an annual copy of our band portrait, signed by each and every one of us. Albums and merchandise are also offered to patrons at a discount on both solstices and equinoxes.
